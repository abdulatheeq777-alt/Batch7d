How It Started

From a Single Store to a Style Movement

It started in 2019 with a small boutique in Hyderabad and a big dream — to make premium menswear accessible to every Indian man. Our founders, tired of choosing between quality and affordability, decided to build something different.

We partnered directly with manufacturers, cut out the middlemen, and passed the savings on to you. Today, Style It Up ships across India, with a catalogue spanning shirts, jackets, bottoms, and footwear — all handpicked for quality and style.

We believe style isn't a privilege — it's a right. And we're here to make sure every man in India can claim it.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

🎯
Quality First
Every product is handpicked and quality-checked before it reaches you. We work only with suppliers who meet our strict standards for fabric, construction, and finish.
💰
Fair Pricing
By working directly with manufacturers and keeping our margins lean, we offer premium products at prices that don't make you wince. Great style shouldn't cost a fortune.
🌱
Sustainability
We're on a journey toward more sustainable fashion. From eco-friendly packaging to partnering with ethical factories, we're committed to doing better for the planet.
🤝
Customer First
Your satisfaction is everything. Easy returns, responsive support, and a shopping experience designed to make your life easier — not harder.
🇮🇳
Made for India
We design for the Indian climate, body types, and lifestyle. Our collections are curated specifically for the modern Indian man — practical, stylish, and built to last.
✨
Inclusive Style
Style is for everyone. We offer a wide range of sizes and fits to ensure every man — regardless of shape or build — can find something that makes him feel great.
